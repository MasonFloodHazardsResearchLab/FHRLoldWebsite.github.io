S2S: Seasonal to Sub-Seasonal Forecast ( Experimental )


This experimental system provides flood forecasts for the Chesapeake Bay region utilizing the hydrodynamic model Advanced Circulation Model (ADCIRC) forced by the atmospheric model Global Ensemble Forecast System (GEFS). The system has been initialized on 4th April to forecast the Water Levels in the Chesapeake Bay for 35 days. 21 ensembles members are run to simulate the water levels in the Bay, Timeseries of Water Levels for 3 stations in the Bay are presented here. The atmospheric model imputs are coming from the SubX Weather System.
